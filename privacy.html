Steady — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Steady is a self-help app for body-focused repetitive behaviors. It is built around a simple privacy promise: your data stays on your device. Steady has no account, no server, and makes no network connections to send your information anywhere.

What Steady stores (on your device only)

  • The behaviors you log (whether you caught an urge or noticed afterward, with optional time, place, mood, situation, and notes).
  • Your settings (tracked behaviors, language, time zone, reminder times, custom tags).
  • Anonymous, on-device usage counts used to improve the app during beta (e.g. how often features are opened). These are counts only — never the content of what you logged.

All of this is stored locally (in an on-device database and the device keychain). It is never transmitted, uploaded, sold, or shared.

What Steady does NOT do

  • No accounts, sign-in, or cloud sync.
  • No servers or APIs — the app makes no network requests with your data.
  • No analytics SDKs, advertising, or third-party trackers.
  • No data collection as defined by Apple (nothing leaves your device).

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled locally by your device's operating system at the times you choose. No notification content is sent through any server.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app removes your logged history and settings. Custom tags you create are intentionally kept in the device keychain so they return if you reinstall; you can remove them anytime inside the app (long-press a custom tag), and they never leave your device.

Children

Steady is a general wellness/self-help tool and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone.

Not medical advice

Steady offers self-help techniques based on Habit Reversal Training. It is not medical treatment and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If your behavior is causing you distress, consider speaking with a qualified professional.
