Common questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Everything you log stays on your device — no account, no servers, nothing is collected or uploaded. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I get reminders?
Open Settings → Check-in nudges, turn them on, and pick the times you want (tap + Add time for a custom one).

How do I remove a custom tag or reminder time?
Long-press it.

How do I change the language or time zone?
Settings → Language or Settings → Time zone.

A note

Steady offers self-help techniques based on Habit Reversal Training. It is not medical treatment. If your behavior is causing you distress, consider speaking with a qualified professional.
